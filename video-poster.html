How to Post Videos

Quick Workflow

  1. Copy Caption - Click the green button on any video card
  2. Open your folder - Navigate to your video files in File Explorer
  3. Go to Instagram/Facebook - Open in another browser tab
  4. Upload video - Drag from File Explorer to the upload area
  5. Paste caption - Press Ctrl+V in the caption field

Posting to Instagram (Desktop)

Option 1: Instagram.com (Easiest)

  1. Go to instagram.com
  2. Click the + (Create) button in the top menu
  3. Drag your video file or click "Select from computer"
  4. Crop/trim if needed, click Next
  5. Add filters if desired, click Next
  6. Paste your caption (Ctrl+V), add hashtags
  7. Click Share

Option 2: Meta Business Suite (Business Accounts)

  1. Go to business.facebook.com
  2. Select your Instagram account
  3. Click "Create Post" or "Create Reel"
  4. Upload your video
  5. Paste caption and schedule or publish

Best for: Scheduling posts, managing multiple accounts, viewing insights

Pro Tip: For Reels, use Meta Business Suite to schedule posts in advance. You can batch upload multiple videos and schedule them throughout the week.

Posting to Facebook

  1. Go to facebook.com
  2. Click "Photo/Video" in the post composer
  3. Drag your video from File Explorer
  4. Paste your caption (Ctrl+V)
  5. Click Post

Using Buffer (Scheduling)

  1. Go to buffer.com
  2. Connect your Instagram/Facebook accounts
  3. Click "Create Post"
  4. Upload video and paste caption
  5. Schedule for optimal posting times

Instagram Video Requirements

Format MP4 (H.264 codec)
Reels Length Up to 90 seconds
Feed Video Up to 60 minutes
Aspect Ratio 9:16 (Reels), 1:1 or 4:5 (Feed)
Resolution 1080x1920 (Reels), 1080x1080 (Square)
Max File Size 4GB

Best Posting Times: Tuesday-Friday, 11am-1pm or 7pm-9pm in your target timezone. Use Buffer or Meta Business Suite to schedule posts.
